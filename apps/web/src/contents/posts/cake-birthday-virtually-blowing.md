## Experience

In today's digital age, celebrating special occasions online has become increasingly popular. A new website, accessible at [Click here](https://hoatepdev.github.io/birthdaycake/), brings a unique and interactive twist to virtual birthday celebrations by offering a customizable digital birthday cake that users can actually blow out!

## Key Features

1. Sensor-Enabled Candle Blowing: The website utilizes your device's audio input to detect when you blow, allowing you to extinguish the virtual candles just like you would with a real cake.

2. Customization Options:

- Personalize the cake with the birthday person's name
- Set the duration for how long you need to blow to put out the candles
- Adjust audio processor settings for optimal performance

3. Festive Confetti: After successfully blowing out the candles, enjoy a burst of virtual confetti. You can even customize the duration of this celebratory animation.
   User-Friendly Interface: The website offers an intuitive design, making it easy for users of all ages to enjoy the experience.

## How It Works

1. Visit the website and grant permission for audio input when prompted.
2. Customize your cake by entering the celebrant's name and adjusting other settings as desired.
3. When ready, start blowing into your device's microphone.
4. Keep blowing until all candles are extinguished.
5. Watch as confetti bursts across your screen, marking a successful virtual celebration!

This interactive birthday cake website bridges the gap between physical and digital celebrations.
